The Left Fork of the Blacksmiths Fork was a gorgeous small stream. I caught 2 fish on it but had several more bites.
From what I can tell this is my first cutthroat trout. I think it is either a Westslope or Bonneville
The trout here like to hide under the brush on the banks. It makes tangles more likely if you keep your flies close to the banks and brush but if you want to catch fish sometimes you have to take risks.
Gorgeous mountains surrounding this stream.
