The Blacksmiths Fork is a great river. Very remote and filled with many trout. The scenery is great and both times I have been in the area I have not seen a single fisherman. Typically the nymphs do well here. The pattern I have been most successful with seems similar to a "Hare's Ear".
I caught my second largest trout here. A big brown that I would guess was around 12-14 inches.
Gorgeous Rainbow trout about standard size for a stream like this. Probably 10 inches.
Great runs to drift your flies in. Just had to be careful because in July the Wasps were all along the banks.
Pulled the mid-sized Ranbow Trout out of this hole. Good deep holes at the end of a run/waterfall seem to produce often for me
