Millcreek Canyon has a beautiful river carving through it. But this was by far the hardest river I have ever fished.
This was very technical fishing. Only had room for bow and arrow casting. I was hoping to catch a Westslope Cutthroat trout.
I only interacted with 2 fish all day. I casted to one I could see for around 45 minutes but I never caught it.
The other trout I interacted with at a different location did take my fly but I lost him and he never took another fly I sent his way.
